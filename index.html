Navigating the Stats program

Major:

  • Math 31 AB, 32 AB & 33 A
  • Stats 10
  • Stats 20
  • Stats 100 ABC
  • Stats 101 ABC
  • Stats 102 ABC
  • 2 Electives
  • 2 Consulting: Stats 140SL & 141SL

Minor:

  • Math 31B
    • In reality, other math classes are necessary since they are the prerequisites for many upper div classes
  • 6 classes from 100, 101 or 102 sequences
    • Option I: Pick any 2 sequences
    • Option II: Pick 2 classes from each sequence
  • 1 Elective

Electives Explained:

  • Pick any upper division stats class
  • These classes can also be selected: Math 131A, 131B, 151A, 151B, 170B, 171, 172A, 172B
  • Stats 105 & 189 do not count

Recommended classes for students planning to study stats in grad school:

  • Math 131A, 131B, 151A, 151B, 170B, 171
